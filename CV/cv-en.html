Case 01 · HEINEKEN Mexico · D&C Solutions
Genie Sales Assistant: sales insights in natural language
From SAP–Databricks integration to AI-augmented analytics: a governed sales data product and an assistant that answers in natural language.
The challenge
Daily OpCo-level sales tracking relied on fragmented reports and processes. Sales data lived in SAP ECC, far from modern analytics capabilities, and getting a concrete answer meant waiting for someone to build a report.
The approach
  • Extraction of sales data from SAP ECC and modeling it as a governed data product in Databricks.
  • Automation of daily OpCo-level sales tracking on top of that data product.
  • Built Genie Sales Assistant on Databricks AI/BI Genie, answering business questions in natural language.
