The challenge

A multi-plant manufacturer managed its supply contracts in a fragmented, manual way. Without a unified view, control was reactive and contractual coverage hard to standardize.

The approach

  • Designed a tool that unifies and standardizes the entire supply-contract process.
  • Moved from manual, reactive management to full, proactive control, with standardized annual contractual coverage.
  • All achieved with no additional infrastructure investment.
