SAP analytics platform modernization

Consolidating and modernizing the entire SAP analytics platform at HEINEKEN Mexico: critical migrations, licensing optimization and leadership of a team of around 15 people.

The challenge

Analytics ran on a legacy SAP landscape, with scattered reporting and a high SAC licensing cost. Sustaining growth required consolidating the platform, modernizing base versions and standardizing executive reporting.

The approach

  • SAC → Analysis for Office redesign with license optimization: −50% in licensing cost.
  • Migration of 300+ reports to SAC, standardizing executive reporting.
  • Critical BW/4HANA 2023 and SAP Data Intelligence migrations delivered smoothly.
  • SAP BO → SAC migration with planning-license optimization.
  • Leadership of a ~15-person SAP Data Engineering team.
